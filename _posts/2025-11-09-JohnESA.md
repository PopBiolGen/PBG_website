PhD student John Ohiolei attended the Entomological Society of America's Annual Meeting held from the 9-12th November at the Oregon Convention Centre, Portland, USA. John delivered a talk titled “Asia to Africa: Containing the spread of an urban malaria vector“, which was based on preliminary results from his PhD project. Over 3,100 attendees from across the world gathered for the conference.
