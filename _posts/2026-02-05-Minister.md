Ben and Judy briefed Matthew Swinbourn, the WA minister for the Environment on the impact of toads, their threat to the Pilbara, and the Toad Containment Zone.  The minister was particularly chuffed (or perhaps disturbed) by our opening gift to him: a stuffed cane toad.
