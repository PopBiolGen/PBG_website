Harry is a recent graduate from Curtin University, having completed a Bachelor of Science majoring in Environmental Science and minoring in Climate change. He has a keen interest in ecology and wildlife conservation, with the goal of contributing to the conservation of Australian wildlife through science and on the ground action.
Harry is undertaking an Honours project looking at different methods of cane toad detection in Northern Australia, with the hopes of developing a standardised method of monitoring cane toads.
